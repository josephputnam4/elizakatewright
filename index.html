Eliza Kate Wright

Playful Exhibit • Artist Profile

About the Artist

Born May 7, 2002, Eliza Kate Wright is an American artist and designer known for blending precision with imagination. A nursing student by day and creative powerhouse by night, Wright approaches every project with a perfectionist’s eye and a playful spirit.

Featured Work: Tyrannosaurus Rex (2026)

This float brings prehistoric wonder roaring back to life. Constructed from lightweight materials and finished with theatrical textures, Wright’s Tyrannosaurus Rex blurs the line between spectacle and sculpture.

“Please do not feed the exhibits.”

Designed and assembled in a residential studio, the float showcases Wright’s signature blend of humor, craftsmanship, and bold imagination. It stands as a reminder that art doesn’t need a museum to feel magical — sometimes it just needs a driveway, a dream, and a dinosaur.
